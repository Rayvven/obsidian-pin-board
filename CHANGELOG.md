# Changelog

## 0.9.0

- **Move a pin to another board.** Right-click any pin → **Move to another
  board…**, pick a folder, and the pin (along with its caption note) is copied
  there. You're then asked whether to keep a copy in the old board too or remove
  it. Anything removed goes to trash, so it's recoverable.
- **Fixed the top bar gap.** The folder dropdown / "All pins" / pin-count bar now
  sits flush against the top of the board — no more slivers of pictures peeking
  through above it while scrolling.

## 0.8.0

- Initial public release: a Pinterest-style masonry board of a folder's images
  and videos, with a folder dropdown and "All pins" view, drag- and paste-to-add,
  a full-size lightbox viewer, per-pin caption notes, auto-refresh on vault
  changes, lazy loading, and settings.
